Ally Almeida!

Hi I'm Ally!

Majors in Aerospace Engineering in the Engineering and Computer Science school of Syracuse University!

Her dream career is to be an astronaut! "Ultimately one day I would love to do that or anything that involves flying planes. More realistically though, my dream career is anything that I can do with my degree. (But working at NASA would be awesome)."
Ally is from Union, New Jerse amd they are of a Hispanic Puerto Rican & Brazilian heritage. The most inspiring person in their life is their mom. “She worked so hard and sacrificed too much to get to where she is today and enjoy life the way she is able to. She has given me the path to continue that and I am more than grateful for that. I hope my kids end up seeing me the same way. (Not in my life, Katherine Johnson)”

About Ally Almeida (she/they)

Ally choose their major because "I have always loved space since I was a child. I attended space camp for what are the best four weeks of my life. I got to fly planes, and even conduct fake space missions where I got to be an astronaut. As the years went on I fell more and more in love with all things aerospace and aviation. I also loved problem solving and math, so everything just kind of came together when learning about a major and a future career in aerospace engineering!”
What brough Ally to Syracuse was she needed a degree to be an engineer, so that is what brought her to college!
