Hi I'm Diana!

Majors in Environmental Studies in the school of Environmental Science and Forestry (ESF)!

Her dream career is to be a professor!
Diana is native to Buffalo, New York. She is of white heritage and the most inspiring person in her life her grandma!


Diana Stone (she/her)

Diana choose their major because “I knew I wanted a career in the environmental field, but I didn't know exactly if I wanted to take the policy route, become a research scientist, work for a nonprofit, etc. Environmental studies was perfect because the coursework is pretty much an even split between environmental science and social science/policy classes. I love my major because I feel like I get a really comprehensive look at all sides of different environmental issues!”
What brought Diana to ESF was that it was perfect for her academically, close enough to home, and affordable!
